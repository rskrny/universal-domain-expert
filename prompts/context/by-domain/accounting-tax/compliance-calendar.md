## FEIE Tracking Rules
- Physical Presence Test: must be outside US for 330 days in any 12-month period
- 2025: 313 days outside US. Qualifying period: Feb 1, 2025 - Jan 31, 2026 (338 qualifying days).
- Rule of thumb: max 35 days in US per year to maintain exclusion
- Any trip to the US counts against the 35-day budget
- If FEIE lost, full US income becomes taxable at standard rates
